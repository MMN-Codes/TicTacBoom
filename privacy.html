Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to TicTacBoom. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our mobile application. We believe in absolute transparency: our game is designed to be completely private.

2. Information We Collect

TicTacBoom is a standalone local multiplayer and AI-based game. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

  • No account registration is required.
  • No background tracking or analytics.
  • Your player names, game scores, and career stats are stored locally on your device and are never sent to our servers.

3. Device Permissions

To provide you with the best gameplay experience, TicTacBoom requests minimal device permissions:

  • Vibration / Haptic Feedback: Used solely to enhance gameplay feel (e.g., when placing an X or O, or achieving a victory). You can toggle this off in the app settings at any time.
  • Internet Access: Used exclusively to check for the latest app updates to ensure you have the most secure, feature-rich version, and to receive developer announcements.

4. Third-Party Services

Currently, TicTacBoom operates without third-party advertisements (like AdMob) or data trackers. Your gameplay remains 100% uninterrupted and private. If this changes in the future, we will update this policy and notify users accordingly.

5. Data Security

Because we do not collect your personal information, there is no risk of your data being breached from our end. All preferences and scores remain safely within your device's secure local storage compartment.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top.
